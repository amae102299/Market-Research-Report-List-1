<p><p>The Human Infrared Thermal Imager market can be classified into two types: Photon Detection and Thermal Detection. Photon Detection technology uses sensors to detect and convert infrared photons into electrical signals, providing a high-resolution image of human body temperature distribution. On the other hand, Thermal Detection technology measures the temperature difference between human body heat and the surrounding environment, creating an image that displays varying heat patterns. These technologies have various applications in healthcare, surveillance, and industrial sectors for detecting abnormalities, ensuring safety, and tracking human presence in low-visibility conditions.</p></p>
