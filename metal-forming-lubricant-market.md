Oil-based lubricants are primarily composed of petroleum-based oils and offer good lubrication and rust prevention. Semisynthetic lubricants are a blend of oil and water, offering enhanced lubrication and cooling properties. Synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered and provide superior lubrication, extreme temperature resistance, and longer tool life.
