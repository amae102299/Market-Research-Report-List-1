<p><p>HALS, or Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers, are additives used in the protection of materials from UV degradation. Polymeric HALS are large molecules that provide excellent UV protection due to their size and complex structure. Monomeric HALS, on the other hand, are single molecules that offer good efficiency but are more soluble and react faster. Oligomeric HALS fall between the two, providing moderate protection while being less soluble than monomeric HALS. Each HALS type offers different characteristics and efficiency, allowing for tailored UV protection in a variety of applications.</p></p>
