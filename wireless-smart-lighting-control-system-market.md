<p><p>The market for wireless smart lighting control systems consists of various types, including Zigbee, WiFi, Bluetooth, Z-Wave, and EnOcean. Zigbee is a wireless communication standard that enables devices to connect and control lighting systems. WiFi allows lighting control through internet connectivity, providing remote access and compatibility with other smart devices. Bluetooth offers short-range wireless control directly via smartphones or tablets. Z-Wave operates on a low-power mesh network, allowing for efficient and reliable lighting control. EnOcean utilizes energy harvesting technology, requiring no batteries or wires for wireless lighting control.</p></p>
