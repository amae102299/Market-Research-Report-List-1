Paste RUTF is a semi-solid mixture that can be directly eaten by children. Drinkable RUTF is a liquid form that can be consumed as a beverage. These different market types allow for varying options to effectively deliver essential nutrients to malnourished children, based on their preferences and health conditions.</p></p>
