<p><p>The Enterprise VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) System market consists of three types: Star Structure, Mesh Structure, and Star and Mesh Structure. </p><p>In the Star Structure, a central hub communicates with multiple remote terminals, which in turn only communicate with the hub. This architecture is suitable for applications requiring a central point of control.</p><p>The Mesh Structure connects all remote terminals with each other, forming a decentralized network. It provides better resilience and redundancy, enabling direct communication between any two nodes.</p><p>The Star and Mesh Structure combines the attributes of both, allowing terminals to communicate directly or through the central hub. This hybrid architecture offers a balance between control and flexibility for large-scale enterprise networks.</p></p>
