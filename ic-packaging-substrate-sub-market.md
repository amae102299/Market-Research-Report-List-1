<p><p>IC Packaging Substrate market types include organic substrate, inorganic substrate, and composite substrate. Organic substrates are made from organic materials such as epoxy resin, offering good electrical insulation and flexibility. Inorganic substrates are made from materials like ceramic or glass, providing excellent thermal conductivity and stability. Composite substrates combine organic and inorganic materials to achieve a balance between electrical and thermal properties. These different substrate types cater to diverse needs in the market, enabling IC packaging to meet various requirements in terms of performance, cost, and reliability in electronic devices.</p></p>
