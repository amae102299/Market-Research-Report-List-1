<p><p>The aircraft plastics market is classified into various types, including PMMA, PC, ABS, PEEK, PPS, and others. PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) offers high transparency and impact resistance, making it suitable for aircraft cabin windows and windshields. PC (Polycarbonate) possesses exceptional strength and heat resistance, commonly used for aircraft instrument panels, exterior lighting, and transparent enclosures. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) provides excellent strength and impact resistance, frequently used in aircraft interiors for panels, trays, and seat components. PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) and PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) are high-performance thermoplastics employed for critical aircraft components, offering excellent mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals and high temperatures.</p></p>
