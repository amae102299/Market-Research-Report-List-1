<p><p>The Medical Polylactic Acid (PLA) market consists of three main types: L-Polylactic (PLLA), D-Polylactic (PDLA), and DL-Polylactic (PDLLA). PLLA is a polymer made from L-lactic acid monomers, whereas PDLA is derived from D-lactic acid monomers. On the other hand, PDLLA is a copolymer formed by combining both L-lactic and D-lactic acid monomers. Each type possesses distinct properties and applications in the medical field, such as drug delivery systems and tissue engineering.</p></p>
