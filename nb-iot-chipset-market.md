<p><p>The NB-IoT Chipset market is segmented into three types: Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone refers to a dedicated spectrum where NB-IoT operates independently. In-Band refers to the utilization of existing LTE spectrum, which shares resources with other services. Guard Band means using the unused spectrum between LTE channels to deploy NB-IoT, avoiding interference with LTE services. These different types cater to various deployment scenarios, providing flexibility and options for integrating NB-IoT technology into existing or new networks efficiently.</p></p>
