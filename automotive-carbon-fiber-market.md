<p><p>The automotive carbon fiber market encompass various types of manufacturing processes used to produce carbon fiber components for automobiles. Resin Transfer Molding involves injecting resin into a closed mold containing carbon fiber reinforcement. Vacuum Infusion Processing uses a vacuum to infuse resin into a pre-placed carbon fiber structure. Injection Molding melts resin and injects it into a mold cavity containing carbon fiber. Compression Molding involves compressing resin and carbon fiber layers in a mold to form the desired shape. Other methods may include Sheet Molding Compound and Prepreg, which require pre-impregnation of resin in the carbon fiber before molding.</p></p>
