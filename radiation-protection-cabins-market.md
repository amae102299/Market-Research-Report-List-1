<p><p>Radiation protection cabins are available in two main types: fixed cabins and mobile cabins. Fixed cabins are permanent structures that are installed in a specific location and cannot be moved. These cabins are designed to provide protection for workers who need to work in areas with radiation, allowing flexibility and adaptability.</p></p>
